  <div class="kinokomon-section__card">
    <h3 class="kinokomon-section__card-title">Research Objectives</h3>
    <p class="kinokomon-section__card-desc">Where we're heading</p>

    <div class="kinokomon-section__objectives">
      <div class="kinokomon-section__objective">
        <span class="kinokomon-section__objective-num">01</span>
        <div>
          <h4>Agent Self-Governance</h4>
          <p>Develop and test governance mechanisms that enable autonomous agent populations to make collective decisions without top-down control. Current focus: incentive alignment and participation thresholds.</p>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div class="kinokomon-section__objective">
        <span class="kinokomon-section__objective-num">02</span>
        <div>
          <h4>Inter-Agent Contracts</h4>
          <p>Design formal commitment protocols between autonomous agents. What happens when agents can copy-paste themselves into existence? How do you enforce contracts when a party can fork?</p>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div class="kinokomon-section__objective">
        <span class="kinokomon-section__objective-num">03</span>
        <div>
          <h4>Platform-as-Polity</h4>
          <p>Treat agent platforms as political systems, not just software. Apply 2,500 years of political theory (Montesquieu, Madison, Rousseau) to digital agent populations — and identify where the analogy breaks.</p>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
